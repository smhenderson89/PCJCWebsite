 Description: Three flowers on one unstaked 33.5-cm inflorescence, five buds on two juvenile inflorescences; sepals and petals oblanceolate, chartreuse overlaid and veined burgundy, petals slightly porrect; lip recurved, undulated edges, light yellow, deep burgundy apical one third, white picotee, keel bright yellow; column cream, sporadic burgundy markings, inferior surface striped burgundy, anther cap white; substance firm; texture matte.
