 Description: Twenty flat flowers and 30 buds on six staked, branched, up to 23-cm inflorescences; 33-cm wide by 35-cm tall plant, 12-cm clay pot, grown in sphagnum moss; sepals and petals gold-chartreuse overlaid magenta, dorsal sepal oval, lateral sepals and petals elliptic; lip white overlaid dark fuchsia, side lobes erect, rhomboid, midlobe spatulate, apex white, callus yellow; column white, flushed light magenta basally, anther cap white; substance firm; texture crystallne; also received award 20255304.
