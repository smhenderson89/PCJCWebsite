 Description: Two striking flowers and three buds on one staked, 64-cm inflorescence; dorsal sepal narrowly deltoid, basal margin strongly recurved, apical margin incurved, cream-yellow, overlaid light green veins, darker centrally; synsepal elliptic, cream-yellow, overlaid light green veins; petals narrowly spatulate, twisting forward apically, cream-yellow, overlaid light green veins, blushed lavender apically; pouch light green, darker green veins, light lavender blush laterally; staminode pale yellow, light green centrally; substance firm; texture satin, pouch waxy.
