 Description: Approximately 780 flowers and 72 buds on 56 upright inflorescences up to 25 cm, four are immature; plant 89 cm x 80 cm, grown in a 15-cm plastic pot; flowers white; sepals and petals apically blushed rose, petals swept forward, overlaid light rose distal half; lip stippled medium rose, keel chartreuse; column and anther cap white; substance very hard; texture crystalline.
