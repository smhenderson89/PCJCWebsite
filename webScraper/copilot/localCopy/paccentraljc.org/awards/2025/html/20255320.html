 Description: Sixteen, well presented non-resupinate flowers and 12 buds on three up to 26-cm unsupported, erect, sequentially blooming inflorescences; sepals and petals narrowly lanceolate, twisted, arranged in a cone shape under the lip, light green, purple blush exterior tips; lip clam-shell shaped, light green broadly striped dark purple, overlaid dark purple distal half, fading centrally distally; column upright light green, anther cap yellow; substance firm; texture diamond dust.
