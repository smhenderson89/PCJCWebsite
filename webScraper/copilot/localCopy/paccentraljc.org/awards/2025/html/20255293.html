 Description: Three stellate, cream flowers on one unsupported arching 47-cm inflorescence; four green bracts extending to the lowest pedicle, emerging from a leaf axil of a fusiform pseudobulb; leaves widest at midlength and slight unequally bilobed; 72-cm wide by 49-cm tall plant grown in bark and perlite, 15- cm diameter black plastic pot; sepals linear; petals narrowly ovate; lip cymbiform, blotched and striated burgundy, yellow centrally, keels yellow; column overlaid blush rose, anther cap pale yellow; substance firm; texture matte; copious nectar noted on inflorescence and flowers.
