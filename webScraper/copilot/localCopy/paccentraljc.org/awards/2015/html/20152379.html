 Description: Two hundred thirty-five flowers and six buds on four double-branched inflorescences plus what are apparently older un-branched inflorescenses with fewer flowers and some buds, on 96 cm high plant (including inflorescences) grown in 20 cm pot; sepals and petals yellow-green almost completely overlaid red-purple, leaving base color exposed as narrow picotee edge and variously as small spots; lip white, three-lobed with lighter red-purple markings; substance firm; texture matte.
