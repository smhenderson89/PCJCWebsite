 Description: One flower on one arching inflorescence; foliage mottled, 14.5 cm long x 3.0 cm wide; dorsal sepal white, veined green, purple suffusion laterally; petals light green with green veins, spots suffused brown; synsepal marked same as dorsal; pouch light green, veined green, brown-pink suffusion at apex and about staminode; staminode horseshoe shaped, light green with green veins centrally; substance light; texture glossy. Recognized for seldom seen species.
