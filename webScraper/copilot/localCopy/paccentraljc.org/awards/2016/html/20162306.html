 Description: Seventy-two scented, well- spaced flowers and three buds on six inflorescences; sepals pale yellow, overlaid and striped mahogany, margins remain pale yellow; petals pale yellow, overlaid and striped mahogany, speckles coalesce into stripes superiorly, margins remain pale yellow; lip light cream heavily spotted dark mahogany, margins solid dark mahogany; column striped and spotted dark mahogany; substance firm; texture matte.
