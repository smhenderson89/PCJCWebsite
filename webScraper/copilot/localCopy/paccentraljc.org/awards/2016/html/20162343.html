 Description: Twenty flowers and 35 buds on 55 sequentially flowering inflorescences produced from upper leaf surface on a pristine 13.4 x 12.6 x 8.0-cm multi-layered plant ball grown on a 13 x 5-cm tree fern plaque, creating a stunning display of a miniature species; leaves ovate, light green, tessellating dark green, reverse purple; flowers minute complex; sepals light green-brown; petals dark red; column purple-red.
