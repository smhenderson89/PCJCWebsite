 Description: Two-hundred fifty-eight flowers and 19 buds on 42 inflorescences plus six immature inflorescences on an 89-cm wide x 67-cm tall plant in a 27-cm pot; sepals and petals paper white, randomly spotted magenta-purple on the adaxial side, visible on the abaxial side; lip paper white, dark magenta-purple on side lobes dispersing to spots on the mid-lobes; substance firm; texture matte.
