 Description: Five hundred twenty-seven ruffled flowers and 108 buds well displayed above the foliage on eight strong multi-branched inflorescences; well-grown plant in 25-cm bulb pan, clean foliage; sepals, pleasantly swept back, and petals bright yellow, barred mahogany; lip bright yellow, central calyx yellow, ridges red, side lobes barred mahogany; column and anther cap yellow; substance firm; texture glossy, lip matte; also received CCE, award #20215328.
