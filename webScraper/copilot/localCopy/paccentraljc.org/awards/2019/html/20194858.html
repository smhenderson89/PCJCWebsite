 Description: Twenty-five flowers and three buds on ten inflorescences; sepals light green, heavily striated burgundy; petals light green, lightly striated burgundy, heavily marked at base; lip cream, lightly marked scarlet, mid lobe recurved; pollen cap cream; substance firm; texture matte; also awarded a CCM of 85 points, award 20194859; awarded as Cymbidium tortisepalum; SITF has determined, with the help of Ken Jacobsen that this is the natural hybrid x nishiuchianum (goeringii x kanran), based on the leaf articulation and the sub equal or shorter inflorescences (May 2019).
