 Description: One striking flower on one 19.5-cm inflorescence; sepals white overlaid light green centrally, boldly striped dark green; petals hirsute, light golden yellow striped dark green, spotted dark mahogany along top edge and randomly at mid-vein and below basally, suffused rose distally; pouch green suffused dark rose, dark green venation; staminode green overlaid dark rose; substance firm; texture matte.
