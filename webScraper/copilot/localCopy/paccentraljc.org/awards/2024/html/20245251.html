 Description: Ten flowers and six buds on three up to 41.5-cm inflorescences; sepals and petals green, slightly veined and suffused burgundy; lip light yellow, keels yellow, midlobe striped and flushed burgundy proximally, burgundy blotch distally, light yellow margin; column light yellow, dorsal surface burgundy stripes and spots proximally, light burgundy flush apically, ventral surface burgundy, anther cap cream; substance medium; texture matte.
