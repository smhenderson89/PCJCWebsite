 Description: Nineteen flowers and two buds on three upright inflorescences up to 19cm tall on a 34-cm tall plant comprised of five tapered bifoliate pseudobulbs, lanceolate leaves in a 21-cm plastic pot; sepals and petals pale ochre overlaid light brown, obscure stripes brown, dorsal sepal porrect, filiform petals recurved; lip pale ochre overlaid brown, porrect side lobes darker distally, keels pale mahogany; anther cap yellow; substance medium; texture matte; recognized for rarity in cultivation and well displayed upright multifloral inflorscences.
