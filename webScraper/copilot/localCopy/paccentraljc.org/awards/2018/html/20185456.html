 Description: Seventeen flowers on two inflorescences; sepals and petals light green boldly striped mahogany, dorsal sepal hooded, petals and lateral sepals falcate; lip white, lateral lobes striped brown, midlobe spotted mahogany, overlaid yellow near margin, central mahogany stripe, callus white, spotted mahogany, heavily ciliate; column white overlaid gold distally, stripped mahogany inferior surface, mahogany on anther cap and anterior margin; substance firm; texture matte.
