Phalaenopsis Wonderful Girl Coco 'AAM5060D'
(Phalaenopsis OX Spot Queen x Phalaenopsis HW-7)
AM 82
Exhibited by: 3H Farm LLC Taiwan Branch

Award 20185478

All awards considered pending until published in the AOS Orchids Plus. Pictures are not the AOS Award photos. Descriptions are subject to revision in final publication.
 Description: Eight shapely, richly colored and boldly patterned Harlequin flowers with high-contrast white lips arranged well on one arching, 81cm inflorescence; sepals and petals largely bright, deep fuchsia leaving a white picotee along margins and small snow white patches basally; lip snow white blushed lightly and speckled with dark fuchsia purple in throat over and around calli; column white, fuchsia blushed distally, darker fuchsia spot on anther cap; substance average; texture matte.
