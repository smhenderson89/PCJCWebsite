Phalaenopsis unregistered hybrid 'FM 7144'
(Phalaenopsis Fangmei Dream Wedding x Phalaenopsis Fangmei Yellow Lippe)
HCC 78
Exhibited by: Fang Mei Orchids

Award 20185464

All awards considered pending until published in the AOS Orchids Plus. Pictures are not the AOS Award photos. Descriptions are subject to revision in final publication.
 Description: Twelve full flowers racked well on one arching 78cm inflorescence; sepals and petals smooth pink-lavender, petals lightly veined with slightly darker lavender centrally; lip midlobe and slightly broadened side lobes pink-lavender, throat magenta-spotted on and near white calli; column white, anther cap white blushed with pink; substance average; texture crystalline.
