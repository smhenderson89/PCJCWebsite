 Description: Six flowers and one bud on one 70-cm arching inflorescence; dorsal sepal arching forward, yellow heavily overlaid dark red, veins and margins very dark; lateral sepals slightly reflexed, yellow heavily overlaid dark red except centrally on each side of very dark central vein; petals reflexed, some curling at side margins, yellow overlaid dark red, darker mid-rib, distal edges yellow; lip white, purple markings, callus white, purple markings, side-lobes yellow, purple markings; column arched, yellow, purple spotting; substance firm; texture matte. AD is awarded for maintaining the erythraeum species charm while introducing intense color from a standard cymbidium.
