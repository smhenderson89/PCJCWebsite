 Description: Three well presented flowers on a 46-cm erect inflorescence; dorsal sepal vibrant chartreuse, heavily striped dark mahogany; synsepal vibrant chartreuse, mahogany stripes; petals symetrically twisted, light green, spotted and barred mahogany basally, coalesced into solid mahogany distally, proximal margins ruffled, sparsely hirsute; pouch light green overlaid mahogany except distal tip; staminode light green-yellow, mahogany cilia edges; substance firm; texture matte, pouch glossy.
