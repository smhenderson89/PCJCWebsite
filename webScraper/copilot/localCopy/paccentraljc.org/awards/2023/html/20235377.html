 Description: Five large, stunning flowers arranged evenly on one 12.5-cm inflorescence; sepals and petals slightly undulate, olive green heavily overlaid cordovan, spotted darker cordovan distal two-thirds, undulate margins; lip cream-white, trilobed, heavily veined dark magenta, mid-lobe broad, fan-shaped, slightly ruffled, throat bright yellow, side lobes closed over column; column white blotched dark magenta; substance firm; texture satiny; commended for size and floriferousness.
