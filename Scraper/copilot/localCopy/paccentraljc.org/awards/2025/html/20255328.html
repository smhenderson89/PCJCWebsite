 Description: Three flowers of exceptional dark color and two buds on one upright 65-cm arching inflorescence; sepals and petals lanceolate, acute, intense dark purple-violet, white proximally; lip tri-lobed, midlobe ovate, acute, recurved apically and marginally, white overlaid pink, midlobe overlaid dark velvety purple-violet distally, throat striped maroon, keels three, yellow, striped maroon dorsally; column white, stippled violet, anther cap white; substance firm; texture glossy.
