 Description: Four hundred fifty-seven flowers and 45 buds on 30 up to 22-cm arched inflorescences; 47-cm by 29-cm plant gracefully blooming on a 7.5-cm by 21-cm wooden mount; flowers golden yellow; sepals slender; petals ovate, slightly rolled on upper margins; lip semi-saccate; substance hard; texture waxy; the plant was immaculate and the overall appearance was improved by the more compact blooming compared with the previous award #20245351.
