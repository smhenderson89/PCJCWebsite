 Description: Eighteen flowers and 14 buds on six evenly arranged up to 81-cm inflorescences; 345-cm circumference plant in a 25-cm plastic azalea pot; dorsal sepal chartreuse, veins heavily marked dark mahogany; synsepal light chartreuse, narrow mahogany veins; petals chartreuse, burgundy spots and veins coalescing to dark burgundy distal half; pouch chartreuse overlaid mahogany, darker mahogany venation; staminode cream, blushed mahogany, hirsuite upper margins; substance firm; texture matte; previous FCC award 20202258.
