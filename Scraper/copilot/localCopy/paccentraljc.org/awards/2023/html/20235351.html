 Description: Twenty-four flowers and five buds on four upright up to 45-cm inflorescences; sepals light green, veined burgundy, infused burgundy concentrated distally; petals light green veined burgundy; lip curled distally, cream, horizontal burgundy barring, side lobes cream, burgundy margins; column light green overlaid and marked burgundy, anther cap yellow; substance firm; texture waxy; sepals appear much darker under photography lights than away from them.
