Phalaenopsis Lin's Genki 'FM 5030'
(Phalaenopsis Sogo Genki x Phalaenopsis Leopard Prince)
AM 84
Exhibited by: Fang Mei Orchids

Award 20185466

All awards considered pending until published in the AOS Orchids Plus. Pictures are not the AOS Award photos. Descriptions are subject to revision in final publication.
 Description: Nine very round and overlapping French Spot flowers and three buds arranged well on one arched 82cm inflorescence; sepals and petals white basally overlaid pink with very fine fuchsia pink spots, heavier centrally; lip side lobes white veined with red centrally, yellow distally, midlobe white with fuchsia pink and yellow blotching, calli yellow overlaid with fuchsia pink spots; column white overlaid with fuchsia pink; substance firm; texture crystalline.
