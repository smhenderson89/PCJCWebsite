 Description: Fifty-nine well arranged flowers on five arching, 75-cm average, inflorescences well presented above gracefully arching leaves; 63-cm x 104-cm plant grown in 17-cm one-gallon plastic pot in a mixture of coconut chips, perlite, bark; dorsal sepal hooded, sepals and petals pale yellow-green overlaid dusty rose, irregular cordovan spots and stripes; lip side lobes light yellow, irregular cordovan markings, midlobe white, irregular burgundy-red markings, keel light yellow, sparsely spotted burgundy red; column cream, inferior surface randomly spotted maroon, superior surface overlaid rose, anther cap white; substance firm; texture matte; also awarded a CCM, #20225408.
