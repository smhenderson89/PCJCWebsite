 Description: Thirteen slightly cupped flowers on one arching 53-cm inflorescence; dorsal sepal pale yellow, midrib finely spotted light burgundy; lateral sepals pale yellow, veins lightly spotted burgundy; petals pale yellow, midrib finely spotted light burgundy; lip white, sidelobes burgundy at margins, midlobe randomly marked large oxblood spots, centrally dark burgundy stripe, keels yellow; column light magenta, anther cap white; substance firm; texture matte.
