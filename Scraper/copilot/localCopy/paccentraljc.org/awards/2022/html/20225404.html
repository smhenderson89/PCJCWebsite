 Description: Approximately 240 flowers and six buds on 24 7.9-cm inflorescences; arising from leaf axils on four fanlike growths on a 7-cm x 5-cm cork mount, leaves 0.6cm wide, 5.5cm long, fine white roots with white hairs, root tips green; flowers translucent white; sepals and petals lanceolate; spadelike lip translucent white; anther cap bright green; pollinia yellow; substance translucent; texture crystalline; light, grass like fragrance.
