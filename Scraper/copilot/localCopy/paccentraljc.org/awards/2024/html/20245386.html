 Description: Fifteen flowers and five buds on one 60-cm erect tri-branched inflorescence; dorsal sepal white overlaid pink, veins slightly deeper pink; lateral sepals white overlaid pink, spotted dark pink basally; petals white overlaid pink, veins slightly deeper pink; lip midlobe forked distally, white overlaid pink, speckled dark pink basally, side lobes clear white, spotted dark purple basally, callus yellow, spotted red; column dark pink, anther cap white overlaid dark pink; substance medium; texture matte; beautiful compact silvery variegated leaves up to 6 cm wide, 28.5 cm long; merited for compact foliage for Phalaenopsis schilleriana.
