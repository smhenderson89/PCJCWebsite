 Description: Thirty-seven flowers and 15 buds on one 31-cm arching inflorescence and one additional inflorescence on keiki off previously flowered inflorescence; sepals and petals white blushed pink; lip white, spotted maroon basally, side lobes two maroon strips centrally, midlobe blushed pink, speckled maroon basal 4/5ths, callus white spotted maroon; column pink, anther cap white; substance medium; texture crystalline; noted for wild-collected native phalaenopsis on Little Orchid Island off southeast coast of Taiwan.
