 Description: Four hundred twenty-two flowers and three buds on 21 cascading branching inflorescences up to 65cm; plant well-flowered, 80 cm wide x 183 cm tall, grown in New Zealand sphagnum and pumice pellets mix in 25-cm plastic round pot; sepals and petals white, intensely flashed purple, more intense centrally, white margins; lip slightly ruffled, white overlaid dark fuchsia, throat intense dark purple; column fuchsia, anther cap white; substance firm; texture matte.
