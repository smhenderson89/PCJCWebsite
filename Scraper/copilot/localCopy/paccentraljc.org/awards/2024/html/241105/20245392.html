 Description: Four hundred forty-eight flowers and 55 buds on 28 cascading inflorescences up to 78 cm; plant exceptionally well flowered, 128 cm tall, 140 cm wide, grown in pumice pellets and New Zealand sphagnum moss mix in 40-cm round plastic pot; sepals white, faintly striated fuchsia; petals white, intensely flashed purple; lip white, overlaid purple, more prominent basally, turning to veins proximally; anther cap white; substance firm; texture matte.
