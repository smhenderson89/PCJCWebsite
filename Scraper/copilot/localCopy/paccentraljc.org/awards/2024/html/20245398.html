 Description: Ten Harlequin flowers on one 75-cm arching inflorescence; sepals white overlaid rose-pink two-thirds proximally, veined darker, intense burgundy picotee; petals rose-pink, veined darker, white tips, burgundy edges, random white-edged fine burgundy spots, more proximally and lower one-third; lip white overlaid burgundy speckles and stripes at base, lobes light burgundy, callus white overlaid yellow and burgundy spots distally; anther cap white; substance medium; texture crystalline.
